The Gin Generator

The Gin Generator is a cocktail discovery tool built for curious drinkers — whether you are a seasoned bartender or someone who just wants to know what to make with the bottles sitting on your shelf.

The idea is simple: enter the ingredients you have at home, and the tool finds cocktails you can actually make. No login, no subscription, no fuss.

The site is built and maintained by Jan Tingström, based in Gothenburg, Sweden. It is a personal project born out of a genuine love of cocktails, good spirits and the craft of mixing drinks.

Recipes are sourced from TheCocktailDB, a free and open cocktail database. The daily tip, articles and translations are written and curated by Jan.

If you have feedback, suggestions or just want to say hello, head over to the contact page.
